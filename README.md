Global AI Workforce Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview
This Tableau project analyzes global AI investment, automation rates, employment trends, and AI readiness across major economies from **2015–2025**.  
It highlights the basic trend of how rapidly AI has grown and how the world has adapted to its increasing influence on jobs, productivity, and workforce dynamics.

Objectives
- Understand global AI investment trends  
- Compare automation and employment rates across countries  
- Analyze job creation vs displacement  
- Visualize AI readiness across major economies  
- Explore how different regions respond to AI-driven transformation  

What I Did
- Built an end-to-end Tableau analytics dashboard
- Cleaned and prepared raw data in Excel 
- Created **KPIs, maps, scatterplots, bar charts, and trend visuals**  
- Added **interactive range slider and filters** for exploration  

Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle  
- **Format:** CSV  

Tools Used
- **Tableau**
- **Microsoft Excel**

---

Key Insights
- **AI investment rose sharply between 2015–2025**, showing rapid global interest and adoption.  
- **Job creation consistently outpaced job displacement**, indicating net positive workforce impact.  
- **Asian countries adapted more slowly to AI** compared to Western nations based on AI readiness and adoption metrics.
